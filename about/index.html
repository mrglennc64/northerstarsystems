— WHY WE EXIST

A deterministic response to dirty data.

Inconsistent fields, broken identifiers, and missing compliance signals cost operators in every industry. NgineAgent is not a database, not a workflow tool, and not a model. It is one engine that validates, corrects, and proves — with machine precision. New verticals are presets: a ruleset and domain config on the same core, never a rebuild.

FOR ACQUIRERS · NGINEAGENT

One deterministic engine. Six products. Five industries.

NgineAgent is the asset: a stateless, deterministic validation and correction engine, already deployed inside six vertical products. This page is the acquisition overview — engine, products, performance, and evidence — without the data room.

— CEO ENGINE OVERVIEW

What you're acquiring.

Most data-quality tools are databases, workflow apps, or models. NgineAgent is none of those. It is a generic validation and correction engine — one codebase that reads raw CSV/XLSX, validates every field against industry rulesets, applies deterministic corrections, and bounces a clean, procurement-ready master with a full evidence chain.

The same engine drives six products across five industries. New verticals are not rebuilds; they are presets — a ruleset and a domain configuration layered on top of the same deterministic core. That is the leverage in the asset: one engine to maintain, many markets to sell into.

It runs inside the customer's infrastructure, with no external calls and no data retention. Same input → same output → same score. No drift, no black box, nothing to explain to a regulator that the evidence chain doesn't already prove.

MASTER — BOUNCE

Every correction, provable.

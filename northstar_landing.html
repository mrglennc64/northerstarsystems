— METHODOLOGY

How Northern Star Systems corrects the record.

Every system in the suite runs on the same four-stage validation pipeline. Metadata enters messy; it leaves trustworthy.

/ 01 — INGEST

Connect any source.

CSVs, DDEX feeds, DSP exports, PRO statements, internal databases. We meet your data where it lives.

/ 02 — VALIDATE

Run rulesets.

Industry standards (ISRC, ISWC, IPI), client‑specific rules, and cross‑reference checks against authoritative registries.

/ 03 — CORRECT

Fix automatically.

High‑confidence corrections happen in the pipeline. Edge cases route to a human reviewer with full context.

/ 04 — REPORT

Audit the chain.

Every correction logged, every change traceable. Compliance‑ready exports and live dashboards by default.
